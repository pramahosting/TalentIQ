About

Business Analyst with 8+ years of experience in data management, analytics, and business intelligence across government, FMCG, retail, healthcare, real estate, and e-commerce sectors. I specialise in turning complex datasets into actionable insights using SQL, Power BI, Tableau, and Python, enabling organisations to make data-driven decisions and achieve measurable business outcomes.

I have a Master’s in Business Analytics from Macquarie University and a strong track record of delivering data governance frameworks, KPI modelling, reporting automation, and system integrations that improve accuracy, efficiency, and transparency across teams.

Core Expertise:

Data Analytics & BI – Power BI, Tableau, SQL, Alteryx, Python (Pandas, NumPy)

Data Science - Machine Learning, Deep Learning, LLMs, NLP, Predictive Analytics

Data Governance & KPI Tracking – Ensuring data quality, consistency, and actionable metrics

Reporting Automation – Streamlining workflows and saving hours monthly

Business Process Analysis – Requirements gathering, documentation, and process mapping

CRM/ERP Integrations – Dynamics 365, Salesforce, and data pipeline automation

Stakeholder Collaboration – Bridging technical and non-technical teams in Agile environments

Passionate about data strategy, governance, and automation, I enjoy building clean, scalable solutions that drive smarter decision-making. I’m currently seeking opportunities as a Business Analyst or Data Analyst, where I can leverage my technical expertise and analytical mindset to deliver real value to dynamic, forward-thinking organisations.

Top skills
