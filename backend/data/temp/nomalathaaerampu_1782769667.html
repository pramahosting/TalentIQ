About

I am a results-oriented Business Analyst with over 20 years of experience across software engineering, requirements engineering, and business analysis, delivering complex solutions in the automotive, mining, telecom, network security, and enterprise digital transformation domains.

My career began with deep hands-on technical roles in embedded systems, networking, and large-scale enterprise platforms, and has naturally evolved into business analysis and requirements leadership. This hybrid background allows me to bridge the gap between business and technology with confidence, translating complex stakeholder needs into clear, actionable functional and non-functional requirements.

I am a CBAP®-certified Business Analyst and an IIBA member, driven by a strong belief in clarity, collaboration, and customer-centric delivery. I enjoy working in environments where structured analysis, thoughtful problem-solving, and continuous improvement are valued.

I am particularly interested in roles where I can leverage my end-to-end delivery experience, technical depth, and business analysis expertise to create meaningful impact.

🤝 Always open to connecting with professionals passionate about business analysis, digital transformation, and data-driven decision-making.
