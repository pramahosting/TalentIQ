About

I am an accountant with a flare for business. I started my career with a Big 4 accounting firm where I worked in accounting and management consulting engagements with large international clients. I later moved to work as a finance manager of a mobile services provider. My career took a turn when I moved to the software industry where I worked in senior management roles in software development, consulting and support functions working with large and small and medium enterprises.

In 2009, I founded Sastra Pty Ltd to provide accounting, consulting and training services to SME clients. We implemented an "embedded accountant" model where our consultants work within our client environment. In addition to providing compliance services, we also act as their trusted advisors and enable them to grow rapidly in a sustainable manner.

We have a great team that has helped the firm grow in terms of clientele and reputation. We have a great clientele that I throughly enjoy working with. Having the right team and right client clientele inspire me to work towards higher goals.
