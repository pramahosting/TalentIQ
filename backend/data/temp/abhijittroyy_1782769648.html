About

I’m passionate about bridging the gap between business and technology helping organisations make smarter decisions by transforming complex data into clear, actionable insights.

With over 3+ years of experience across data analytics, process optimisation, and business intelligence, I’ve worked across procurement, IT services, sports analytics, and retail.
