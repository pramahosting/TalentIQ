    Signs You're Definitely an Accountant 😂

    You might be an accountant if...
    ✅ You check formulas even when Excel already did the math.
    ✅ You reconcile before you relax.
    ✅ You trust nobody's numbers until you've checked them yourself.
    ✅ You spend 30 minutes looking for a discrepancy worth less than your morning coffee.
    ✅ You get oddly excited when everything balances on the first try.
    ✅ You save a file as:
    "Final_Report.xlsx"
    "Final_Report_v2.xlsx"
    "Final_Report_v2_FINAL.xlsx"
    "Final_Report_v2_FINAL_REALFINAL.xlsx"
    ✅ You double-check totals even after checking them three times already.
    ✅ You treat a perfectly organized spreadsheet like a masterpiece.

    People think accounting is all about numbers.

    But accountants know it's really about patience, attention to detail, and a healthy amount of trust issues. 😂

    “Must have NetSuite experience.”
    I see this a lot—and I get it. Familiarity feels like a safe bet.

    Let’s talk about accounting systems! Because I love them.

    I’ve seen too many great accountants get passed over just because they haven’t used a specific system listed on a job posting.

    Here’s the thing: If someone has experience with any accounting system—and truly understands accounting—they can learn yours. Quickly.

    I’ve worked in Intuit QuickBooks Desktop, QuickBooks Online, Restaurant365, MEDITECH 5.66, Meditech 6.1, and Infor. I have audited many more! Each one looks different, but the core principles stay the same. Give a strong accountant a day or two, and they’ll be navigating your ERP like a pro.

    So next time you’re hiring an accountant, don’t just ask, “Have you used our system?”
    Ask, “Have you used an accounting system?”

    That small shift could open the door for incredible talent.

    #SupportAccountants #AccountingLife #SystemFluency #HireSmart #ERPExperience #RecruiterTips #FinancialLeadership #Don’tOverfilter #InclusiveHiring
