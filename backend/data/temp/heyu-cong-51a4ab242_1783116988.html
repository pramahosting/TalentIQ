About

I’m an accounting professional based in Sydney with experience in public practice accounting, specialising in taxation, compliance, financial reporting, and client advisory services.

I hold a Master of Professional Accounting and a Master of Commerce (Finance) from the University of New South Wales (UNSW), along with a Bachelor’s Degree in Economics. Over the past few years, I have developed practical experience across individual and business taxation, BAS, FBT, payroll compliance, ASIC corporate compliance, bookkeeping, and financial statement preparation for a diverse range of clients.

Most recently, I worked as a Tax Accountant at AMA Business Services, where I gained hands-on experience across taxation, compliance, and client advisory services for individuals and businesses. My work involved preparing and reviewing tax returns and financial reports, managing complex taxation matters, liaising with the ATO, and supporting clients with ongoing compliance and operational needs.

I’m passionate about continuous learning and building a long-term career in accounting and advisory, with a strong interest in taxation, business services, and client relationship management.

Outside of work, I enjoy sports, travelling, road trips, trekking, and NBA basketball.

Feel free to connect — I’m always happy to network with professionals in the accounting and finance industry.
